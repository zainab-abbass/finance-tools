Costs are modelled bottom-up: your operating cost base is split into payroll and other operating expense and grown separately, so EBITDA margin moves with your actual assumptions rather than being held flat. Inflation partially erodes gross margin; operating cash flow is estimated as EBITDA less a 10% working-capital drag on revenue growth. The sensitivity band flexes growth and cost assumptions by the points you set. These are directional planning estimates, not a forecast of actual results or financial advice.
