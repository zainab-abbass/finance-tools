A monthly cash model: operating cash (revenue less payroll and opex) is adjusted for the change in working capital each month, where debtor, creditor and inventory days move from current toward target over the first year. Runway is the point at which cash plus your credit facility would be exhausted. The credit facility is treated as available buffer. These are directional planning estimates, not a forecast of actual results or financial advice.
