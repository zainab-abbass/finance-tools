Lifetime value sums each year's revenue weighted by the probability the customer is still active (from churn or lifespan), with annual value growing by upsell plus price increase and an optional referral uplift. Profit LTV applies your net margin; CAC payback uses contribution margin (gross margin less service cost). These are directional planning estimates, not a forecast of actual results or financial advice.
